Tension Tunnel

About

Tension Tunnel is a casual, minimalist and challenging game that will put your focus, reflexes, and nerves to the test.

The game has simple mechanics but it requires high dexterity and precise execution from the player.

The goal is simple: avoid all obstacles and reach the portal to go to the next level.

Download it now and start feeling the tension. Be careful, it's highly addictive!
